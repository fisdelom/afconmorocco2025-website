About the App

The AFCON Morocco 2025 Unofficial Fan App is your essential guide to the Africa Cup of Nations. Follow every match, explore the host cities, stay connected with fans, and enjoy a complete travel and event experience.

Unofficial App Disclaimer

This is an unofficial fan app. Not affiliated with CAF or AFCON organizers. Created by independent fans to enhance your AFCON 2025 experience. We are not endorsed by or connected to CAF, AFCON organizers, or any official tournament entities.

Main Features

  • Match schedules and results
  • Team profiles and statistics
  • Player profiles and statistics
  • Live updates and notifications
  • Travel and event information
  • Host city information

Tournament Information

Dates: December 21, 2025 to January 18, 2026

Host Cities: Rabat, Casablanca, Tangier, Marrakech, Agadir, Fez

Teams: 24 national teams competing for the title
