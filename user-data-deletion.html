Delete My Data — Afcon Morocco 2025

Use this page to delete all personal data associated with your Afcon Morocco 2025 app account. Choose the option that fits you: in-app deletion (if available) or an email request.

Quick options

1 — Delete inside the app

If you have the Afcon Morocco 2025 app installed and the account deletion feature enabled, follow these steps:

  1. Open the app → Settings → Account.
  2. Tap Delete Account / Delete My Data.
  3. Follow the on-screen confirmation steps.

After confirmation your profile, predictions, and preferences will be permanently removed. You will receive an email confirmation when deletion is complete.

Email our support team at support@afconmorocco2025.com or use the form below.

2 — Request deletion by email (instant mailto)

If you prefer we delete your data manually, send a verified request. Use the form to prefill an email you can send from your device.

Support email: support@afconmorocco2025.com. We will respond and process verified requests within 30 days.

Automatic & scheduled deletion

Accounts inactive for 12 months may be automatically removed. Aggregated analytics and anonymized logs may be retained for product improvement and legal compliance.

Data we may keep

  • Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics
  • Audit logs retained for legal compliance (when required)

What happens after deletion?

  • You will receive an email confirmation when deletion is complete.
  • Your predictions, profile information and preferences will be permanently erased.
  • Deleted accounts cannot be recovered.

If you have questions about data portability, objections, or want a copy of your data before deletion, contact us at support@afconmorocco2025.com.
